IBM Elite Support offers enterprise support for OpenRAG to help you develop and deploy OpenRAG applications with confidence.

IBM Elite Support for OpenRAG is a subscription to the OpenRAG expertise at IBM. It's meant for OpenRAG users who want all the benefits of running their own open-source deployments, as well as the peace of mind that comes with having direct access to the team that has authored the majority of the OpenRAG code.

IBM Elite Support subscribers can get help with general-purpose and technical questions for their open-source OpenRAG deployments. If an issue is encountered, IBM is there to help.

To subscribe or learn more, see [IBM Elite Support for OpenRAG](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/announcements/elite-support-openrag-delivers-enterprise-support-users-need-confidently-create-deploy-openrag-applications).
